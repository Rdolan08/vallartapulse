This unique place is close to everything, making it easy to plan your visit to PV's Zona Romantica. Located in historical Old Vallarta's Downtown with ground and roof-top pools and 24/7 security. Amazing views everywhere!
Apartment is fully furnished and has all the living essentials.
Perfect for warm & sunny vacations with your partner, friends or family in a magical beach destination
Enjoy the beautiful emerald beaches of Banderas Bay and all that the heart of Puerto Vallarta has to offer!

The space
Flexible 1-bedroom unit with private balcony fully furnished with outdoor dining and lounge furniture.

The unit is in a brand new building, Oasis Vallarta, with some of the best rooftop views in the city with river, ocean, and jungle views - a 360 not to miss, particularly at sunset.

Your private balcony on the 8th floor continues the best views in all of Vallarta in the comfort of your own space.

A quick walk to the beach, nightlife, and Vallarta food scene, Oasis is tucked away along the river to allow for a peaceful and quiet return home.

IMPORTANT: FOR THIS UNIT PARKING IS LIMITED AND IT IS OUTSIDE THE BUILDING BY THE SECURITY POST AT ENTRANCE (STILL IN PROPERTY, NOT REGULAR STREET). IT IS FIRST COME FIRST SEVES AND IT IS SUBJECT TO AVAILABILITY.
